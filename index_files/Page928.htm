“scientific fund (SF)
"System and financial analysis
based on cognitive modeling technology"
named after N.A. Prokopenko””

scientific-organizational divisions (II),
administrative-economy divisions (III),
scientific-research and educational activity
financing divisions (IV),

applied scientific researches financing divisions (IV.B),
divisions of scientific centre (additional) (V),
scientific-historical divisions (additional) (VI),
scientific-publical divisions (additional) (VII)
 divisions of “SIO "Academy of cognitive
natural sciences"” (additional) (VIII)
and other divisions.
