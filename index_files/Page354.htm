3. Data about Nikolay Andreevich Prokopenko

Nikolay Andreevich Prokopenko –
senior officer (colonel) of Armed forces of RF,
military scientist, teacher and builder
in the field of the system and financial analysis
of bridges, tunnels and construction designs,
and also difficult engineering constructions,
in particular: elevated and surface,
underground and underwater.
He worked at chair “Constructions of bridges and tunnels”
of “The Command and engineering faculty”
(earlier “Automobile and road faculty”)
of “Military academy of the back and transport”
(now “Military academy of material-technical support
named after general of army A.V. Hrulyov”)
of The Ministry of defence of RF
(before The Union of the soviet
socialist republics – USSR,
also is later than The Russian soviet Federal
socialist republic – RSFSR).
He was engaged in the complex construction
of military and civil bridges, crossings
and other buildings and constructions,
and also training of qualified specialists
of transport service with the highest
professional education
for performance of problems of the technical
service and restoration of bridges and tunnels.
He participated in creation
modern and perspective
technologies and designs
for temporary and short-term
restoration of bridges and tunnels,
and also for the device of the floating
bridges and ferries.
He was constantly improved preparation
of officer shots, introducing the advanced
methods and tutorials in educational process
listeners and cadets.
He was successfully carried out
the educational-methodical and scientific-research work,
in particular: he was developed and published
a large number of monographs, textbooks, manuals,
scientific articles and scientific reports
for The Ministry of defence of RF
(including under a signature stamp
“Confidentially (Secret)”).
He has the state awards of USSR and RF
(military awards, medals and other).
Other information is provided
on demand in The Ministry of defence of RF.
